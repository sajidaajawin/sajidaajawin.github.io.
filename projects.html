Arduino:

With a strong command of Arduino programming and electronics, I have honed my skills in designing and developing innovative projects. From creating custom sensors and automation systems to crafting interactive gadgets, my ability to harness the potential of Arduino boards has enabled me to bring my creative ideas to life. I take pride in my hands-on experience in coding, circuit design, and troubleshooting, allowing me to tackle complex challenges and engineer practical solutions. My passion for Arduino as a personal skill reflects my commitment to pushing the boundaries of technology and realizing inventive concepts, I created a project that works on solar energey and hot water using Arduino and Heat sensors by Program it to Open the water tank when its tempreature arrives to 25 celsius
